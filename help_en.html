‚òÄÔ∏è Times of Day

Since Casual time only uses 1-12, these words are often added for clarity:

  • morgens: Morning (05:00 - 09:00)
  • vormittags: Late morning (09:00 - 12:00)
  • mittags: Noon (12:00 - 13:00)
  • nachmittags: Afternoon (13:00 - 18:00)
  • abends: Evening (18:00 - 23:00)
  • nachts: Night (23:00 - 05:00)
